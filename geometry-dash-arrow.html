About Geometry Dash Arrow

Inspired by the iconic Geometry Dash and its legendary wave mechanic, Geometry Arrow brings a bold, standalone experience that’s just as punishing — and even more hypnotizing. 

What Is Geometry Arrow?

At its core, Geometry Arrow is a rhythm-driven, wave-mode platformer where you control a sleek, sharp arrow icon flying through deadly mazes. The twist? You don’t just jump — you ride the wave. Tap to switch direction, hold to soar, and release to dive in a perfectly timed zigzag across increasingly brutal levels.

It’s fast. It’s chaotic. It’s addictive. And every second tests your mastery of timing, pattern recognition, and raw concentration.

How to play

Left Click / Tap: Hold to fly up, release to fall.

Navigate through spike pits, floating platforms, rotating blades, and disorienting visuals.

Zigzag with precision — the arrow never stops moving, and neither can your brain.

Survive six levels of escalating difficulty, from mild mayhem to full-on demon-tier insanity.

Level Breakdown

Level 1 – Easy Start: Learn the ropes — gentle obstacles and a slower pace.

Level 2 – Getting Real: Longer levels, more spikes, still friendly… mostly.

Level 3 – Hard Mode: Narrow paths and increased speed demand a sharper reaction.

Level 4 – Harder: Now you’re dodging at double time. A true skills test begins here.

Level 5 – Insane Difficulty: Dark maps, unpredictable traps, and zero forgiveness.

Level 6 – Demon Level: Fast, furious, and frantic. Only elite players survive this finale.
